    Shooting games are an action-packed genre that focuses on firearms and ranged weaponry, immersing players in intense combat scenarios where quick reflexes and strategic thinking are crucial. This genre encompasses a variety of subgenres, including first-person shooters (FPS), third-person shooters (TPS), and light gun games, with popular titles like Call of Duty, Counter-Strike, and Halo leading the way. Players engage in fast-paced gameplay, navigating detailed environments while aiming to eliminate opponents, complete missions, or achieve high scores. Shooting games often feature competitive multiplayer modes, allowing players to face off against each other in various maps and game types, as well as single-player campaigns that provide rich narratives and engaging challenges. With their combination of adrenaline-fueled action, realistic graphics, and immersive sound design, shooting games remain a favorite among gamers seeking excitement and adrenaline in their gameplay experience.
